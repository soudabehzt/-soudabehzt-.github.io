Classic coffee-soaked ladyfingers layered with creamy mascarpone.

Ingredients

  • 300 g ladyfingers
  • 500 g mascarpone
  • 3 eggs (or 300 ml whipping cream)

Steps

  1. Whisk yolks with sugar; fold in mascarpone. Whip whites (or cream); fold.
  2. Quickly dip ladyfingers in coffee; layer with cream. Repeat.
  3. Chill 4+ hours; dust with cocoa before serving.
