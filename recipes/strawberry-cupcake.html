Strawberry Cupcake

‚è± 45 minüçΩ 12 servingsüßë‚Äçüç≥ Medium

Moist vanilla cupcakes topped with fresh strawberry frosting.

Ingredients

  • 180g all-purpose flour 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 5g baking powder 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2.5g salt 1/2 tsp salt
  • 115g butter, softened 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 150g sugar 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs 2 large eggs
  • 5ml vanilla extract 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 120ml milk 1/2 cup milk
  • 150g fresh strawberries, pureed 1 cup fresh strawberries, pureed
  • 500g powdered sugar 4 cups powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F and line cupcake pan.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla.
  5. Alternate adding flour mixture and milk.
  6. Fill cupcake liners 2/3 full and bake 18-20 minutes.
  7. Cool completely before frosting.
  8. Make frosting by mixing butter, powdered sugar, and strawberry puree.
  9. Frost cupcakes and garnish with fresh strawberries.
