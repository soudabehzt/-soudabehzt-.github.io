Creamy Lemon Chicken

Tender chicken in a rich, tangy lemon cream sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts, pounded thin
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until golden.
  4. Remove chicken and set aside.
  5. Add garlic to the same pan and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add cream, lemon juice, and zest. Simmer 3-4 minutes.
  7. Return chicken to pan and heat through.
  8. Garnish with parsley and serve.
