Classic homemade pizza with fresh dough and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • Your favorite toppings
  • Cornmeal for dusting

Steps

  1. Mix yeast with warm water and let sit 5 minutes.
  2. Combine flour and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Add yeast mixture and olive oil, knead until smooth.
  4. Let dough rise in oiled bowl for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat oven to 500¬∞F with pizza stone.
  6. Roll out dough on cornmeal-dusted surface.
  7. Add sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
  9. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
