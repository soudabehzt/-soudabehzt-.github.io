About me

Welcome to SouChef, a space where technology meets innovation in the kitchen. I’m Soudabeh Torbati, an engineering student with a passion for blending creativity, design, and everyday life into projects that feel both smart and inspiring. SouChef is more than a recipe collection, it’s my creative playground. Here, I experiment with interactive design, coding, and smart-kitchen concepts, turning ideas into projects that are as functional as they are beautiful. It’s a portfolio of what happens when engineering meets imagination.

Within these pages, you’ll find curated recipes, soft visuals, and thoughtful projects that reflect my eye for detail and my love for problem-solving. Whether you’re here to discover a new dish or to see how I approach design and development, I hope SouChef sparks both inspiration and curiosity.
